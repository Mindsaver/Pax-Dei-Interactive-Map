Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Data Collection

We collect the following data:

  • Markers that you add to the map, including their location and any text you associate with them.

Data Usage

The data collected is used to provide you with the ability to save and share markers on the map.

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using local storage. This means your data is not transmitted to our servers.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties.

User Rights

You have the right to delete your data at any time. You can do this by using the "Clean Markers" function on the map, which will remove all stored markers.
